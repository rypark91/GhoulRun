Glowing Ghoul Run

Run!

Test your running prowess and your bravery! With little to no light to go off of, random scares, and the opportunity to embody the spirt of Halloween and competition! Feel free to a simple 5k, an in the middle 10k, or the challenge of half a marathon. All at night! You up for the challenge?

Information

  • Place: 13th West State Street at Ghostly Park
  • Date and time: October 25th, 7pm. Some time after sun set.
  • Water stations at certain checkpoints
  • Flashing strobe light warning
  • Prizes and refreshments at the end of the races
  • Feel free to dress up! Because we have a costume contest at the end of each race.
